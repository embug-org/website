Bernd Bauer
===========

My name is Bernd Bauer, and I’m, so to speak, a founding member
of this regulars table. I’m also the one who usually organizes
the regulars table meetings and makes the table reservations.

I have been an embedded systems developer for many years.
I currently work at `VusionGroup <https://www.vusion.com/>`_.
There, I work with Bluetooth, embedded systems, Embedded Linux,
and many other things.

Open source and free software are very important to me personally.
From my professional experience, I’ve seen that the world wouldn’t
function the same way if it weren’t for open source.
Of course, open source relies on communities—including our small
EMBUG community. I’m a very sociable person who values social interaction
highly. That’s why I attend almost every regulars table.

My interests are:

* Open source. I'm a big fan of open source.
* Embedded systems (including microcontrollers, real-time operating systems
  such as Zephyr, and, of course, Embedded Linux)
* Mobile communications (amateur radio, LoRa, Meshtastic, Bluetooth)
* IT Security
* Privacy Policy
